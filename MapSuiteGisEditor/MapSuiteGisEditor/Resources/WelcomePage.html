How to Use Map Suite Explorer

  • Step 1: click "New Project" button on the upper left corner.
  • Step 2: add contents:
      by clicking the "Content" button
      by clicking the "Base Map" button
      by pressing Ctrl+O
      by dragging and dropping any supported files(shp, ecw, sid)
  • Step 3: try other buttons freely by yourself.
  • Step 4: save project by clicking the "Save Project" button next to the "New Project" button.
  • Step 5: close the application.
